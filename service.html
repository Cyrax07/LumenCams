Our Excellent CCTV Security Services

CCTV

Installation

At LumenCams, we specialize in providing high-quality CCTV installation solutions to help protect your home or business. Whether you need surveillance for residential, commercial, or industrial spaces, we offer customized CCTV systems that meet your specific security needs.

CCTV

Configuration

CCTV configuration refers to the process of setting up your CCTV system to ensure that cameras, recording devices, and monitoring features function as expected. This involves configuring camera settings, network settings, recording schedules, motion detection, and remote access.

CCTV

Maintenance

Maintaining your CCTV system is crucial to ensure it continues to operate effectively and provides reliable surveillance coverage over time. Regular maintenance helps identify and fix potential issues before they become major problems, ensuring that your system provides consistent security.

CCTV

Repair & Service

CCTV repair and service is a critical part of maintaining a surveillance system. Over time, even the best CCTV systems may experience issues such as camera malfunctions, poor image quality, storage problems, or network failures. Regular maintenance and quick repairs can help ensure that the system remains operational

CCTV

Monitoring

CCTV Monitoring refers to the active or passive surveillance of video footage captured by closed-circuit television cameras. It involves observing live video feeds or recorded footage for security, safety, and investigative purposes. CCTV monitoring can be done manually by security personnel or remotely using automated software.

CCTV

Access Control

CCTV Access Control refers to the integration of surveillance cameras (CCTV) and access control systems (ACS) to enhance security at entry points, such as doors, gates, or other controlled access points. The integration of these two systems allows for a more robust security solution by not only monitoring activity.
